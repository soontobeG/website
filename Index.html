FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your first detail with us.

Do you bring your own water and power? +
Yes! Our mobile units are fully self-contained. We bring water tanks, generators, and all professional equipment. You don't need to provide a single thing.
How do I get the 50% founding member discount? +
No code or voucher needed — the 50% discount is automatically applied during checkout for all customers until the first 100 founding spots are claimed. The prices you see on our pricing page are already the discounted prices.
How long does a detail take? +
The Base takes ~1.5 hours, The Standard ~3 hours, and The Works ~4 hours. Times may vary slightly based on vehicle size and condition.
What if it rains on my appointment day? +
We monitor weather closely and will proactively reach out to reschedule if needed. Exterior services require dry conditions, but interior-only packages can proceed rain or shine.
How do I pay? +
We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Venmo, and Zelle. Payment is collected after the service is complete and you've approved the results.
Is my vehicle safe with your technicians? +
Absolutely. All CarsPro technicians are background-checked and we are fully insured and bonded. Your vehicle and property are protected every step of the way.
